Thanks for downloading Blender Grpahite, below are some simple instructions for installing these theme into your own copy of blender; it's super simple, trust me!

1. Download the theme from the releases section if you haven't already.
2. In Blender in the “Preferences” window switch to the “Themes” section.
3. Press the “Install” button and specify the downloaded theme file, locate Blender_Graphite_1.xml
4. Press the “Save Preferences” button to apply your brain new UI theme.
5. Enjoy!
